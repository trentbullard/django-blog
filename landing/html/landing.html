About

I'm a determined, hard-working developer that never backs down from a challenge. I love deconstructing code and finding optimizations. I've been coding since the 10th grade (2002), starting with C++. To this day, my burning passion for software engineering has not wavered. Computer systems are second nature to me and I have never encountered a computer problem I wasn't able to solve.

My primary skillset includes all stages of development from database design and implementation to advanced coding in Ruby (on Rails), C#(.NET frameworks & core), Python (2 & 3), JS (Node), and Java to UX design in React, Django, Razor, SCSS, and more. I also have professinal experience with both iOS and Android mobile app publishing. As a hobby, I have become proficient in 3D modelling (Blender), graphic design (Vector), and music making (LMMS).
